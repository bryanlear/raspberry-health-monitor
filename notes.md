### Omhs Law

**Defines relationship between:**
- Voltage
- Current
- Resistance

$$E = I * R$$

or

$$volts = amps * ohms$$

Therefore,
- higher resistance resuces current
- higer voltage increases current (while $\Omega$ **constant**)
- In power distribution constant **Power** applies

---

A voltage divider can be used to reduce voltage (but used for **signals**):
```
5V signal → voltage divider → 3.3V signal
```

$$Vout = Vin × R2 / (R1 + R2)$$
